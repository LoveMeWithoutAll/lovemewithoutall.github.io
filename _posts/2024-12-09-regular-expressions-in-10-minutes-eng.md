On this project I had no choice but to use regular expressions a lot, but fortunately times have changed, and AI wrote me some elegant regex. Still, when things go too smoothly, it tends to make you uneasy. The first regex book I finally cracked open turned out to be wonderfully approachable. I'll probably forget most of what's in this book before long, but if I read it again, surely it will all come back to me quickly, won't it? A book I wish I had read much sooner.

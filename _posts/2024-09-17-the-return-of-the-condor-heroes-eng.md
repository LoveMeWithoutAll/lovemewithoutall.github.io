The characters in this novel keep going blind. They are blinded by mist and clouds, blinded by flames and smoke, blinded by grudges and revenge, blinded by the passing of years and time. Beyond these, the veils that cloud a person's eyes are beyond counting: honor, the will to win, greed, and more. Among them, the most beautiful veil is, without a doubt, love. What is this thing called love, that it should decide between life and death?

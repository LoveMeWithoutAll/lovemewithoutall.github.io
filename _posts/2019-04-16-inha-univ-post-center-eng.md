The photo below is the entrance to the Inha University mail sorting room.

It is located on the 1st floor of the Inha University Student Union building (Biryong Plaza), one floor below Hana Bank.

When you receive international mail from AliExpress via Korea Post EMS, it gets delivered here.

It wasn't easy to find!

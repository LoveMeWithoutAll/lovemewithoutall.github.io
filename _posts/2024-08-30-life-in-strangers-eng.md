I live in the heart of the city, yet I still love to observe living things other than humans. It's because I hold out hope that I might draw some kind of inspiration from their lives, which seem unbound by anything and yet pursue the utmost efficiency. That is why coming to understand the intentions and stories embedded in each of their footprints and traces is a kind of moving experience for me. Now that I've come to know so much more about their traces, I look forward to seeing what more I'll be able to notice from here on.

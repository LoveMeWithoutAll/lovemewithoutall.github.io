[Tasmanian Tigers Are Extinct. Why Do People Keep Seeing Them? ](https://www.nytimes.com/2021/03/10/science/thylacines-tasmanian-tigers-sightings.html?smid=url-share)

Below is a summary of the article.

It has been nearly 80 years since the Tasmanian tiger went extinct. And yet, sightings of this animal keep being reported. There are even photos and videos. But every time they are checked, they turn out to be a dead end. Why does this keep happening? Because of confirmation bias. We see what we want to see. If you go searching for evidence to support a claim, you will soon find it. Because that is what you want.

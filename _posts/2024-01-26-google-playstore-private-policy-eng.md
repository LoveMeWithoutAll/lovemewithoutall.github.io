I received an email from the Google Play Store. It said that if I didn't take action within a month, they would pull the app I'd made from the Play Store... Since it was an app I'd left unattended for a long time, I went into the management console, and it turned out Google had already been warning me about this for quite a while.

After searching around, I found that the [Privacy Portal](https://www.privacy.go.kr) used to offer a service that automatically generated a privacy policy (which is long...), but they discontinued that service at the end of 2023. So I had to write one myself.

That said, this app doesn't collect any personal information at all, and it doesn't even require signing up. So after reading through the [Privacy Policy Drafting Guidelines](https://www.privacy.go.kr/front/per/inf/perInfStep01.do), I enlisted the help of GPT-4 and [Google Sites](https://sites.google.com/new).

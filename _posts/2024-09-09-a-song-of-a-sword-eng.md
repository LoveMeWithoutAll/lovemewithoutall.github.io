Reality always appears far away, hazy in the distance. But what ought to be stands right beside me. Closing the gap between the two is hard. Where should I remain? Where should I press forward? Within that ugly gap, every choice is always agonizing. The sword's tip trembles in anguish. All I can do is steel my heart.

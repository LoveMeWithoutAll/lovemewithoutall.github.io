I bought the [V9](https://ko.aliexpress.com/item/V9-Bluetooth-5-0-touchless-PK-Z8/32992975093.html?spm=a2g0s.9042311.0.0.15e64c4dItoIjM) on AliExpress. It's my first pair of bone conduction headphones.

Here are the pros and cons I noticed after using it for 2 days.

## Pros

1. It doesn't block your ears.
1. It's cheap(?). I bought it for $32.00, and as of 2019/04/18 it's $24.
1. The packaging is neat.
1. It stays securely on your temples, even during vigorous exercise.
1. It doesn't hurt or feel uncomfortable even after wearing it for a long time.

## Cons

1. A lot of sound leaks out. So much that you can hear it well even without pressing the headphones against your head. Someone even asked me if I had bought a speaker.
1. The sound quality isn't good.
    - Compared to the [EarPods](https://www.apple.com/kr/shop/product/MNHF2FE/A/35mm-%ED%97%A4%EB%93%9C%ED%8F%B0-%ED%94%8C%EB%9F%AC%EA%B7%B8-earpods) and the [Sony SBH70](https://support.sonymobile.com/kr/stereobluetoothheadsetsbh70/userguide/function-overview/#gref), the sound quality is poor.
    - Compared to the [Sony SmartBand Talk SWR30](https://www.sonymobile.com/kr/products/smart-products/smartband-talk-swr30/#gref), the sound quality is slightly worse.
1. The play/pause button intermittently doesn't work.
1. It catches on glasses, which is uncomfortable.
1. It gets sweaty.
1. You can't lean back on a chair that has a headrest or neck rest.

## Other

1. Continuous playback time is about 5 hours, and charging takes about 2 hours.
    - The [AliExpress link URL](https://ko.aliexpress.com/item/V9-Bluetooth-5-0-touchless-PK-Z8/32992975093.html?spm=a2g0s.9042311.0.0.15e64c4dItoIjM) says it supports 6 hours of continuous playback.
    - The manual included with the product says it supports 5 hours of continuous playback.

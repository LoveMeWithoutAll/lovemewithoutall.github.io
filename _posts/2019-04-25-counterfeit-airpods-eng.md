I bought a pair of fake AirPods with a cloned W1 chip from AliExpress. The conclusion is...

## Don't buy them

# Unboxing

## Appearance

* When I opened the box, a cheap paper smell hit me right away..
* No stickers included
* The packaging is loose...
* There's glue smeared on the case
* ~~No charging cable included~~ Like the genuine AirPods, it was wedged in some weird spot, so I almost threw it away
* The charging cable doesn't connect well..
* The AirPods look identical to the genuine ones
* Looking closely, there's a gap in the assembly seams
* The case has that knock-off tint to it

## iOS Connection

* Won't connect to iPhone X
* Won't connect to iPad Pro 10.5
* iOS version 12.2

## Android Connection

* Connects to Android devices
* The right earbud is louder
* There's a gap in the left earbud's assembly seam, so sound seems to leak out
* As for calls... I don't even want to try
